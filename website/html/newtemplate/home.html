Welcome to Star Clash

Star Clash is a multiplayer game hosted by Columbia College.

It's a team-based action strategy game. A remake of the classic space shooter which takes advantage of the modern networking technoogy to let people play together.

To play just download the game, register an account and run the executable!

If you have any complaints or suggestions visit the contact tab.

To view any player's stats, just head to the stat page and browse via user name.
